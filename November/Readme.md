### Applications

The Kaye Validator AVS and ValProbe RT systems are designed for qualification and validation of a wide range of thermal processes and controlled environments, including:

- **Steam Sterilizers (Autoclaves):** Validation of sterilization cycles and temperature uniformity.  
- **Dry Heat Sterilizers:** Monitoring high-temperature depyrogenation or dry heat processes.  
- **Steam-in-Place (SIP) Systems:** Validation of steam sterilization directly in process piping or vessels.  
- **Water Cascade / Water-Fall Sterilizers:** Ensuring uniform heat distribution in water-based sterilization systems.  
- **Incubators:** Verifying controlled temperature and humidity conditions for cell growth or storage.  
- **Stability Chambers:** Mapping long-term stability testing environments.  
- **Freezers:** Temperature mapping of low-temperature storage and cryogenic units.  
- **Freeze Dryers (Lyophilization):** Monitoring and validating temperature and pressure during the freeze-drying process.  
- **Process Vessels:** Measuring and validating temperature uniformity in large tanks.
